About Misbar

Refining hydrocarbon solutions for Saudi industry.

Misbar Energy refines, processes and supplies hydrocarbon feedstocks that support industrial growth in Saudi Arabia and across the region. From raw materials to refined products, we focus on safety, reliability and consistent quality.

Process-driven refining operations Local production with regional reach

Our Commitment

We operate with disciplined process control, modern manufacturing assets and an integrated supply chain. This allows us to deliver hydrocarbon products that meet the expectations of domestic and regional clients in multiple industrial sectors.
